TPGS Storage Conditions for 04/22/2026 -- INTRADAY


Notice Text:

Due to intraday nomination changes for Gas Day April 22, 2026, the following restrictions will be in place on the Tres Palacios Gas Storage, LLC (TPGS) system for Gas Day April 22, 2026:

Tres Palacios has scheduled and sealed injections. No increases in injections will be accepted.

Additionally, the following restrictions will remain in place for Gas Day April 22, 2026:

Tres Palacios has restricted authorized injection overrun nominations. No increases to authorized injection overrun nominations will be allowed except to comply with NAESB bump guidelines.

Tres Palacios has restricted IWS rate schedule nominations with the exception of nominations from meter 40332 - PERMIAN HWY PIPELINE (TPGS1130R), 45301 - TEXAS EASTERN (TPGS1120B), and 45307 - FLORIDA GAS TRANSMISSION (TPGS1080B). No increases to IWS rate schedule nominations with the exception of nominations from meter 40332 - PERMIAN HWY PIPELINE (TPGS1130R), 45301 - TEXAS EASTERN (TPGS1120B), and 45307 - FLORIDA GAS TRANSMISSION (TPGS1080B) will be allowed except to comply with NAESB bump guidelines.

Customers are advised that capacity may become available as the nomination and confirmation process continues throughout the day.

Please contact your Operations Account Representative if you have any questions.
