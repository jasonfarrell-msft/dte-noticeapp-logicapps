NEXUS Force Majeure


Notice Text:

Due to DTE unplanned system maintenance, Nexus Gas Transmission, LLC (NEXUS) hereby declares a Force Majeure in accordance with Section 15 of the General Terms and Conditions of its FERC Gas Tariff.  NEXUS has been informed that this unplanned maintenance will impact the Willow Run, Milford Junction and Belle River Segment's beginning Gas Day March 7, 2026, and until further notice. 

 

NEXUS will post updates to the status of DTE unplanned system maintenance as soon as they are known.

 

Please contact your Operations Account Representative if you have any questions.
