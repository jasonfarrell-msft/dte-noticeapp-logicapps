NEXUS DTE Unplanned Outage


Notice Text:

Nexus has been notified by DTE of unplanned system maintenance effective ID2 Cycle, Gas Day March 7, 2026, and until further notice, that will impact the Willow Run, Milford Junction, Washington and Belle River Segment's.  Nexus is working with various parties to minimize impacts to scheduled volumes for gas day March 7, 2026.

 

This repair will result in the following restrictions on the NEXUS system:


DTE-Willow Run Segment - March 7 and until further notice:


NEXUS capacity thru the Willow Run Segment on DTE Pipeline will be reduced from 1,151,000 Dth/d to approximately 1,032,000 Dth/d (capacity will vary based DTE's allocation of capacity to NEXUS). Potential impacted primary delivery points include N4237 (DTE) Vector Milford Junction-DEL, N4035 (DTE) Union St. Clair-Del, and N4ST1 (VEC) St. Clair (US) as well as any secondary point on DTE Pipeline or Vector Pipeline downstream of the Willow Run Segment on DTE Pipeline.

 

DTE-Milford, Washington and Belle River Segment - March 7 and until further notice:

 

NEXUS's total capacity thru Milford and Belle River will be reduced from approximately 606,000 Dth/d to approximately 505,000 Dth/d (capacity will vary based DTE's allocation of capacity to NEXUS).  NEXUS's Deliveries (including Primary Firm deliveries) to N4ST1 (VEC) St. Clair (US) and to N4035 (DTE) Union St. Clair-Del will be impacted based on this reduction in capacity.
