Delhi Compressor Station Unplanned Outage


Notice Text:

Due to loss of commercial power, Southeast Supply Header, LLC (SESH) has experienced an outage at its Delhi, Mississippi Compressor Station and repair efforts to restore this compressor station to full capacity are underway. 

 

As a result of this outage, SESH's mainline capacity will be limited to approximately:

 

Delhi:                 891,000 Dth/d

Gwinville:          939,000 Dth/d

Gwinville East:  939,000 Dth/d

Lucedale:           936,000 Dth/d

 

In addition, meter 83002 (Enable Gas 24") and 83003 (Gulf South) will be required to be scheduled to 0 Dth/d until power is restored.

 

SESH will reduce scheduled quantities to the above capacities effective for Gas Day January 26, 2026.

 

For Gas Day January 25, 2026, at the end of the ID-2 confirmation, SESH will seal the capacity through the Delhi Compressor station.   No incremental capacity will be scheduled through Delhi after ID-2.

 

SESH will post updates to the status of the repairs as soon as it is known.

 

If you have any questions regarding this posting, please contact your Operation Account Representative.
