SESH Imbalance Notice -- UPDATE


Notice Text:

Southeast Supply Header, LLC ("SESH") has limited operational flexibility and as a result, effective 9:00 AM CCT, January 24, 2026, SESH requires all delivery point operators to keep actual daily takes out of the system equal to or less than scheduled quantities regardless of their cumulative imbalance position unless otherwise coordinated with your operations account representative.

If necessary, SESH will utilize any provision of its tariff to ensure system integrity including restricting nominations for underperformance.

Correspondingly, effective 9:00 AM CCT, January 24, 2026, the previously posted imbalance notice is no longer in effect.

This notice will remain in effect until further notice.

Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
