Manta Ray Critical Notice - March 2026 Outage Update


Notice Text:

The work currently underway on Manta Ray at Ship Shoal 332 and Ship Shoal 207 is still progressing on schedule. Manta Ray expects the work to be finished sometime between Sunday, March 22 and Tuesday, March 24. Manta Ray will post a critical notice once the work has been completed.

The Nautilus-B SS207 (MR037) and Kinetica Deepwater Express-B (MR039) delivery meters combined are still limited to an estimated 105,000 mcf per day.

Platform operators are still expected to ensure that actual produced volumes align with scheduled quantities.

The Ewing Banks 910 (MR809) production will remain shut in for the duration.

The following production sources are not impacted.

Green Canyon 18 (MR812)
Ship Shoal 349 (MR803)
GC 65 Bullwinkle (MR802)
GI-116 Hickory (MR032)
EW-1003 Prince (MR811)
ST-295 (MR034)

Additional information will be provided as it becomes available.

For questions concerning this notice, please contact your Offshore Account Representative at 713-627-6204.
