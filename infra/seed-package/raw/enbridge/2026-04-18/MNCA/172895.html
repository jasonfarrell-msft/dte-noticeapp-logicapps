MNCA Imbalance Notice -- UPDATE


Notice Text:

Maritimes & Northeast Canada (MNCA) has limited operational flexibility to manage imbalances. Effective 9:00 AM CCT, January 21, 2026, MNCA requires all shippers and point operators to carefully review demand for gas and schedule gas consistent with daily needs and to tender and receive gas consistent with confirmed nominations regardless of their cumulative imbalance position.

Correspondingly, effective 9:00 AM CCT, January 21, 2026, the previously posted imbalance notice is no longer in effect.

Shippers are required to coordinate any imbalance payback through MNC's account representative and actual payback quantities may be limited depending on system operations and conditions.

If necessary, MNCA will utilize any provision of its tariff to ensure system integrity including the issuance of customer specific or system wide OFOs.

Please contact your Operations Account Representative if you have any questions.
