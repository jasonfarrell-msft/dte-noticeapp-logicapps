ETNG Operational Flow Order -- UPDATE -- EFF 2/3


Notice Text:

Effective 9:00 AM CCT, February 3, 2026, ETNG is lifting the Balancing Alert Operational Flow Order for the following operational areas issued January 24, 2026:

 

i)                   all meters on Line 3200 between Tracy City and Topside including all meters on the 3500 Line,

ii)                 all meters east of the Rural Retreat Compressor Station that are on the 8" and 12" system but excluding meters on ETNG's 3600-1 Line.

 

ETNG requires any imbalance payback be coordinated through your Operations Account Representative.  If necessary, ETNG will utilize any provision of its tariff to ensure system integrity including the issuance of customer specific or system wide OFOs. 

 

Additionally, the previously posted Hourly Customer Specific OFO and System Wide OFO will continue to remain in effect.
