ETNG Action Alert -- EFF 4/18


Notice Text:

In order to maintain the operational integrity of the system, ETNG is issuing an Action Alert pursuant to Section 14.6 of the General Terms and Conditions of ETNG's FERC Gas Tariff effective 9:00 AM CCT, April 18, 2026, for all LMSMA and LMSPA parties.

This Action Alert does not affect the ability of ETNG to receive or deliver quantities of gas for scheduled nominations to any customer, storage field, or pipeline.

During the effectiveness of this Action Alert, balancing parties under Rate Schedules LMSMA and LMSPA must be balanced such that actual deliveries of gas out of the system must be equal to or greater than scheduled deliveries out of the system and actual receipts of gas into the system must be equal to or less than scheduled receipts into the system.


The penalty provisions under Section 47.5(b) of the General Terms and Conditions of ETNG's FERC Gas Tariff shall apply for failure to conform for each dekatherm of actual receipt quantities that are greater than scheduled receipt quantities and for each dekatherm of actual delivery quantities that are less than scheduled delivery quantities, in each case with a tolerance of 5% of scheduled quantities or 500 Dth (whichever is greater).

 

The penalty will be subject to $0.98 for each Dekatherm of gas by which it deviated from the requirements of the Action Alert. In addition, ETNG will not permit retroactive nominations to avoid an Action Alert penalty.

This Action Alert will remain in effect until further notice.


Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
