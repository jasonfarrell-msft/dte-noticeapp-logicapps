ETNG Customer Specific Hourly Operational Flow Order -- EFF 1/24


Notice Text:

In order to maintain the operational integrity of the system, East Tennessee Natural Gas (ETNG) is issuing an Hourly Balancing Alert Operational Flow Order (OFO) pursuant to Section 14.7 of the General Terms and Conditions of ETNG's FERC Gas Tariff effective 9:00 AM CCT, January 24, 2026, for certain delivery point parties on the ETNG system.  ETNG will be contacting the affected parties.

 

This OFO does not affect the ability of ETNG to receive or deliver quantities of gas for scheduled nominations to any customer or pipeline.

 

During the effectiveness of this OFO, the notified parties under Rate Schedule LMSMA must be balanced such that actual deliveries of gas out of the system must be equal to or less than scheduled deliveries out of the system for each hour. Notified balancing parties will be charged in accordance with the penalty provisions under Section 47.5(b) of the General Terms and Conditions of ETNG's FERC Gas Tariff shall apply for each hour that each dekatherm of actual delivery quantities are greater than day-ending scheduled delivery quantities divided by 24 multiplied by 1.3 (i.e. 30% tolerance), or 500 dekatherms (whichever is greater).  In addition, ETNG will not permit retroactive nominations to avoid an OFO penalty. 

 

This Hourly OFO will remain in effect until further notice.

 

Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
