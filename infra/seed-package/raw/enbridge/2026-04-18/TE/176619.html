AGT/TE Mahwah (00201/78201) and Ramapo (00214/7814) Changes Effective 5/1/2026 Gas Day


Notice Text:


Download File: Mahwah Ramapo Change Effective May 2026.pdf

Currently the Mahwah and Ramapo interconnect with AGT includes TE customers that utilize the NJNY Lease, and they are confirmed separately by each specific business unit. Starting 5/1/2026 Gas Day we will now confirm and utilize receipt rankings across the AGT and TE customers at these interconnects. Nominations that are received from 00201 on AGT and 78201 on TE and separately 00214 on AGT and 78214 on TE will be aggregated into a single volume for confirmation (one volume for 00201/78201 and one for 00214/78214). Reductions that are applied will be processed between AGT and TE based on the Receipt Ranking inputted by the customer. If rank is the same or left as 999 it will be prorated among the customer's AGT and TE Contracts.
