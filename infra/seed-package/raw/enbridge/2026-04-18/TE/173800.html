TE Operational Flow Order -- UPDATE


Notice Text:

In order to maintain the operational integrity of the system, TE is updating the previously posted Operational Flow Order (OFO) pursuant to Section 4.3 of the General Terms and Conditions of TE's FERC Gas Tariff effective 9:00 AM CCT, February 10, 2026, to all OBA and Transportation Contracts, with the exception of those governed by a FERC gas tariff, in Texas Eastern's Market Area Zones M2-24, M2-30 and M3.

This OFO does not affect the ability of TE to receive or deliver quantities of gas for scheduled nominations to any customer or pipeline.

 

The penalty will be equal to three times the daily Platts Gas Daily "Daily Price Survey" posting for the High Common price for the geographical region, as defined in Section 8.5(a) of the General Terms and Conditions of TE's FERC Gas Tariff for the day on which such violation occurred.  TE will not permit retroactive nominations to avoid an OFO penalty except for shippers who can acquire day ending Due Shipper supply for No Due Pipe OFO's and Due Pipe supply for Due Shipper OFO's.

 

Shippers in Texas Eastern's Access Area Zones ETX, STX, ELA and WLA as well as Market Area Zones M1-24 and M1-30 are required to coordinate any imbalance payback through TE's account representative and actual payback quantities may be limited depending on system operations and conditions. If customers do not adhere to imbalance requirements stated above, TE will utilize any provision of its tariff to ensure system integrity including the issuance of customer specific or system wide OFOs.

 

Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
