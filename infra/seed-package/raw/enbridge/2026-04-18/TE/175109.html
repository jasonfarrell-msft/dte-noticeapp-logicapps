TE Action Alert -- LIFTED EFF 3/12


Notice Text:

Effective 9:00 AM CCT March 12, 2026, Texas Eastern (TE) is lifting the previously posted Action Alert for Market Area Zones M2-24, M2-30 and M3.

 

Shippers across all Texas Eastern's Access Area Zones and well as Market Area Zones are required to coordinate any imbalance payback through TE's account representative and actual payback quantities may be limited depending on system operations and conditions. If customers do not adhere to the imbalance requirements stated above, TE will utilize any provision of its tariff to ensure system integrity including the issuance of customer specific or system wide OFOs.

 

Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
