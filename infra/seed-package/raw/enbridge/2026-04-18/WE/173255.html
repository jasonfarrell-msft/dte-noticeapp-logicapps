Station 2 Force Balance Job Run Revision - Effective Gas Day Monday, February 2, 2026


Notice Text:

Westcoast will be adjusting the time of the Station 2 Trading Pool (WA202) force balance job run from 6:00pm MT (19:00 CT) to 7:00pm MT (20:00 CT), effective gas day Monday, February 2, 2026.


Shifting the time from the last Batch Validation job of the ID3 cycle to the first Confirmation job of the ID3 cycle, allows time for Interconnect Delivery Operator confirmation reductions to be applied prior to the force balancing job run.


As a result, shippers will not see force balancing cuts at Station 2 (FCC and/or MCC reduction reasons) applied until the 7:00pm MT (20:00 CT) job run.


Final scheduled quantities for the ID3 cycle remain available after the final Confirmation job of the cycle at 8:35pm MT (21:35 CT).


If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to the Gas Scheduling team at westcoastgasscheduling@enbridge.com or (403) 699-1600.
