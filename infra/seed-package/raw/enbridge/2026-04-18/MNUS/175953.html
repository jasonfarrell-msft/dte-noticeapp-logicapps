MNUS Imbalance Notice -- Update


Notice Text:

Maritimes & Northeast (M&N) has limited operational flexibility to manage imbalances. Effective 9:00 AM CCT, April 3, 2026, M&N requires all shippers and point operators to keep actual daily takes out of the system equal to or greater than scheduled quantities regardless of their cumulative imbalance position unless otherwise coordinated with your operations account representative.

Correspondingly, effective 9:00 AM CCT, April 3, 2026, the previously posted imbalance notice is no longer in effect.

Additionally, M&N continues to require all Power Plant Operators to provide information mandated by FERC Order No. 698 until further notice. Information required includes the hourly consumption profile of directly connected power generation facilities.

To the extent shippers and/or point operators do not adhere to this requirement,
M&N will utilize any provision of its tariff to ensure system integrity including the issuance of customer specific or system wide OFOs.

This notice will remain in effect until further notice.

Please contact your Operations Account Representative if you have any questions.
