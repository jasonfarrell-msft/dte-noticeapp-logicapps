MNUS Pipeline Conditions for 01/27/2026


Notice Text:

For Gas Day January 27, 2026, Maritimes & Northeast (M&N) has approved and scheduled nominations at each pipeline segment and meter station up to M&N's operational capacity. The following location has been restricted due to requested nominations exceeding M&N's operational capacity:

M&N has restricted a portion of interruptible nominations sourced from points north of Westbrook for delivery to points south of Westbrook. No increases in nominations sourced from points north of Westbrook for delivery to points south of Westbrook will be accepted.

The No Due Pipe System Wide OFO issued January 24, 2026, will remain in effect until further notice.

Additionally, M&N requires all Power Plant Operators to provide information mandated by FERC Order No. 698 until further notice. Information required includes the hourly consumption profile of directly connected power generation facilities.

Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
