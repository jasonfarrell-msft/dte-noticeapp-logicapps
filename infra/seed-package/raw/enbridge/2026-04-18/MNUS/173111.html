MNUS Pipeline Conditions for 1/24/2026 -- INTRADAY


Notice Text:

Due to intraday nominations changes for Gas Day January 24, 2026, Maritimes & Northeast (M&N) has approved and scheduled nominations at each pipeline segment and meter station up to M&N's operational capacity. The following location has been restricted due to requested nominations exceeding M&N's operational capacity:

M&N has scheduled and sealed nominations sourced from points north of Westbrook for delivery to points south of Westbrook. No increases in nominations sourced from points north of Westbrook for delivery to points south of Westbrook will be accepted.

M&N continues to require all shippers and point operators to keep actual daily takes out of the system equal to or less than scheduled quantities regardless of their cumulative imbalance position unless otherwise coordinated with your operations account representative.

Additionally, M&N requires all Power Plant Operators to provide information mandated by FERC Order No. 698 until further notice. Information required includes the hourly consumption profile of directly connected power generation facilities.

Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
