TPGS Operational Advisory


Notice Text:

Due to anticipated high injection nominations, Tres Palacios Gas Storage (TPGS) may have limited ability to utilize compression to facilitate interruptible wheeling activity and authorized overrun injections.  

 

Furthermore, effective Gas Day March 10, 2025 TPGS will review all:

-IWS activity originating from interconnects other than 40332 - PERMIAN HWY PIPELINE, 45323 - MATTERHORN PIPELINE, 45307 - FLORIDA GAS TRANSMISSION and 45301- TEXAS EASTERN.

-All authorized overrun injection activity.

 

Restrictions to interruptible rate schedules and authorized overrun injections may be required until further notice.
