AGT Customer Specific Operational Flow Order -- EFF 1/22


Notice Text:

 

Algonquin Gas Transmission, LLC (AGT) has limited operational flexibility to manage imbalances.  Therefore, in order to maintain the operational integrity of the system, AGT is issuing an Operational Flow Order (OFO) pursuant to Section 26 of the General Terms and Conditions of AGT's FERC Gas Tariff, effective 9:00 AM CCT, January 22, 2026, for certain delivery point parties on the AGT system.  AGT will be contacting the affected parties.

 

This OFO does not affect the ability of AGT to receive or deliver quantities of gas for scheduled nominations to any customer or pipeline.

 

During the effectiveness of this OFO, all parties must be balanced. The penalty shall apply to each dekatherm of actual quantities that exceeds the greater of 500 Dth or 105% of scheduled quantities for Due Shipper balances, as well as each dekatherm of scheduled quantities that exceeds the greater of 500 Dth or 106% of actual quantities for Due Pipe balances. The penalty will be equal to three times the arithmetic average of daily Platts Gas Daily "Daily Price Survey" posting for the High Common price for "Algonquin, city-gates" for the day on which such violation occurred as indicated in AGT's General Terms and Conditions Section 26.8.   In addition, AGT will not permit retroactive nominations to avoid an OFO penalty except for primary firm no-notice nominations or shippers who can secure day ending Due Shipper supply for Due Pipe imbalances and Due Pipe supply for Due Shipper imbalances.

 

This Customer Specific Operational Flow Order will remain in effect until 9:00 AM CCT January 23, 2026.

 

Please contact your Operations Account Representative should you have any questions.
