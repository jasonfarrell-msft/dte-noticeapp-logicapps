Subject: Columbia Gulf Transmission, LLC (“Columbia Gulf”) Flexible Balancing PAL Open Season #2026-0318
 
 
 
 
Columbia Gulf is soliciting bids from interested parties for a monthly balancing service under its existing PAL Rate Schedule* (“PAL”) at CGT Mainline Pool (P20) under the following terms:
 
 
TERM:                                                                                       April 1, 2026– October 31, 2026**
 
Maximum Total Parked/Loaned Inventory (MIQ):                           +/- 200,000 Dth***
Maximum Daily Parked/Unparked Quantity:                                              20,000 Dth
Maximum Daily Loaned/Returned Quantity:                                              20,000 Dth     
Location:                                                                                              CGT Mainline Pool (P20)
 
*Please note PAL is an interruptible service
**Shipper’s PAL Inventory must be zero by the end of term
***PAL Inventory can be taken negative up to the MIQ
 
Columbia Gulf will receive bids beginning Wednesday March 18, 2026, at 09:00 a.m. Central Time (“CT”) through Wednesday March 25, 2026, 12:00 p.m. CT.
 
Columbia Gulf notes that the maximum applicable Recourse Rate for the capacity posted in this open season is the Total Effective Rate (maximum base tariff rate plus all applicable surcharges) for Rate Schedule PAL, as set for in the Tariff. 
 
Participation in the Bidding Process – All bids submitted in this open season will be treated in a nondiscriminatory manner pursuant to the terms posted in this open season.  To participate in the bidding process, interested parties must send an email with bid terms to:
 
Diana_Roberts@TCEnergy.com
 
All bids should refer to Open Season # 2026-0318 Columbia Gulf Flexible Balancing PAL
 
 
All bids should be submitted in the form of a monthly rate due Columbia Gulf.
 
To participate, all interested parties must have a fully executed MASTER PAL Agreement in place with Columbia Gulf. If you do not have a MASTER PAL Agreement in place and would like to participate in this open season, please contact the Columbia Gulf representatives below.
 
A bid must contain all of the applicable information required by the Bid Form at the end of this posting.  Bidders must satisfy creditworthiness in accordance with Columbia Gulf’s FERC Gas Tariff (“Tariff”).  A potential shipper may submit multiple bids, each higher than its preceding bid, for all or any portion of the capacity or term of service made available by Columbia Gulf. If a bidder submits multiple bids, Columbia Gulf will consider only the last, highest bid submitted by bidder for the purpose of awarding this open season.
Assessing Bids- Columbia Gulf reserves the right to reject any bids that do not conform to the terms listed herein.  Columbia Gulf reserves the right to reject any bid that is incomplete or includes conditions or provisions that Columbia Gulf determines, in its reasonable discretion, to be unacceptable. Columbia Gulf reserves the right to reject any bid or request that contains modifications to the Bid Form or conflicts with Columbia Gulf’s Tariff.  Columbia Gulf has the right to clarify bids.
 
 
Awarding of Service –– Columbia Gulf will notify successful bidders no later than 2:00 PM CT, Wednesday March 25, 2026.   Any awards under this open season will be made independently based upon objective criteria and the economic value of the bid to Columbia Gulf.  Columbia Gulf will award the Flexible Balancing PAL included in this open season to the shipper with the highest calculated total monthly rate due Columbia Gulf, submitted in the form of a monthly rate (bid per dth of MIQ) for the term.  However, any bid above recourse rate for Rate Schedule PAL will be treated as a bid at the maximum recourse rate for Rate Schedule PAL.  If two or more bidders submit bids with equal monthly rates, and which are conforming to the terms of this notice, and there is insufficient capacity available to serve all such bidders, then Columbia Gulf shall determine the successful bidder based on the order in which the bids were received.
