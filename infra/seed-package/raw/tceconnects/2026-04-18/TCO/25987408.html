Subject: Line-H Maintenance
Author: Dillon Schoolcraft
Notice Text
Columbia Gas Transmission, LLC (TCO) notifies customers of upcoming maintenance activities on Line H scheduled to begin on Wednesday, April 1, 2026. As a result, the following meters will be reduced to zero total capacity effective Timely Cycle for Gas Day April 1, 2026, and continuing until the work is complete:
821917 – A&W-Gandee
840254 – Vandalia
842446 – Spencer H Line Sales
Based on current scheduled volumes, the anticipated impact to firm service is less than 2,000 Dth/day. 
Additionally, during the first phase of the outage (tentatively scheduled April 1-16, 2026), meter 833237 (behind MLI CNR18) will be required to physically shut in. Therefore, effective Timely Cycle for Gas Day April 1, 2026, CNR18 will be reduced to zero non-firm.
TCO encourages customers to monitor the Construction and Maintenance Schedule and the Daily Capacity Posting for any non-firm adjustments related to this maintenance.
Reservation charge credits (RCCs) will be determined per the process set forth in the General Terms and Conditions, Section 38 of TCO’s FERC Gas Tariff (“Tariff”). However, TCO will only issue RCCs if TCO is unable to schedule or deliver primary firm nominated capacity as a result of the outage. In the event RCCs are issued as set forth in the Tariff, shippers will receive RCCs based on its 7-day average usage quantity less any scheduled quantities on the contract. By means of this notice, TCO is establishing which specific Gas Days will be included when determining a shipper’s 7-day average usage quantity pursuant to the provisions in the Tariff.
Please contact your Customer Services Representative with any questions.
